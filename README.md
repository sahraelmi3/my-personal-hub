# My Personal Hub 🌸

A beautiful personal productivity app with login, notifications, and multi-user support!

## Deploy to Vercel

Click this button to deploy:

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=YOUR_GITHUB_URL)

## Features

- 🔐 Multi-user login system
- 📅 Calendar & scheduling
- 📞 Work/call tracking with scripts
- 💰 Budget tracking
- 📝 Notes with AI
- 🔔 Notifications & reminders
- 💜 Beautiful pink/purple aesthetic

## Setup

1. Click "Deploy to Vercel" button above
2. Connect your GitHub account
3. Deploy!
4. Open the URL on your iPhone in Safari
5. Add to Home Screen

Your data saves on your device!
